Dungeon Duels

A strategic dungeon-based game that combines AI-driven characters, combat tactics, and resource management.

Dungeon Duels Project Overview

Dungeon Duels is a dynamic RPG game where players assemble a team of characters, each with unique abilities, to explore dungeons, fight enemies, and collect loot. The game features a range of AI types, including Medics, Assassins, Hunters, and more, each with distinct behavior patterns that enhance the strategic depth of the game.

Players start with a single character and can expand their roster to up to six, each contributing different skills and stats. The game emphasizes tactical decision-making, resource management, and real-time combat, making each dungeon run a unique challenge.

Key Features:

  • Diverse AI Characters: Each AI type has a specific behavior, such as Medic, Assassin, Hunter, and Looter, allowing players to build varied strategies.
  • Unique Items and Skills: Players can equip characters with items like bows, spears, and wands, and unlock skills such as Stealth, Enrage, and Necromancy.
  • Strategic Combat: Combat is driven by AI behavior and player tactics, requiring thoughtful planning and adaptation to enemy moves.
  • Resource Management: Collecting and managing items, orbs, and gold is crucial for upgrading characters and improving their abilities.

Game Mechanics:

  • AI Roles: Medics heal allies, Assassins target from behind, Hunters keep distance, and Looters prioritize resource collection.
  • Character Upgrades: Purchase new characters and items from the in-game shop using collected resources.
  • Team Customization: Mix and match characters to form a balanced team that can handle various dungeon challenges.
  • Power-Up Orbs: Special orbs scattered across dungeons can temporarily boost character stats or activate powerful skills.

Items and Skills:

  • Items: Bows, Daggers, Healing Wands, Health Packs, etc., each with specific effects.
  • Skills: Incite enemies, boost damage, recruit MOBs, and more, adding layers of strategy to the gameplay.

Project Details

  • Technologies: Unity, C#, AI Algorithms
  • Date: October 2024
  • Category: RPG, AI, Game Development
