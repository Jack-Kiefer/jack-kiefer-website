MTG AI

Exploring the boundaries of AI with a custom-built environment for Magic: The Gathering.

MTG AI Project Overview

This project involves designing an OpenAI Gym environment to enable an AI to learn and play a simplified version of the popular card game, Magic: The Gathering. The goal is to utilize multi-agent reinforcement learning techniques to teach the AI how to make strategic decisions in a highly complex, multi-dimensional environment.

Using Python and popular AI libraries such as TensorFlow and OpenAI Gym, I created a simulation of a basic MTG match. The environment includes a variety of features and complexities found in the original game, enabling the AI to learn through experience and adapt its strategy over time.

Key Features:

  • Custom OpenAI Gym environment for MTG
  • Implemented multi-agent reinforcement learning
  • Focused on decision-making and strategy development

Project Details

  • Technologies: Python, TensorFlow, OpenAI Gym
  • Date: August 2023
  • Category: AI, Game Development
