Pokemon AI Project Overview

This project focuses on developing an AI capable of simulating and strategizing in Pokemon battles. The AI is trained to understand various combat styles and adapt its approach based on the opponentâ€™s behavior and environmental factors. This project leverages reinforcement learning techniques to build an AI that not only reacts but also anticipates and plans strategies in complex, multi-layered battle scenarios.

Using Python, TensorFlow, and custom AI algorithms, the AI learns from battle data, refining its tactics over multiple iterations. The AI models different roles, such as Medic, Assassin, and Hunter, to simulate realistic gameplay and decision-making.

Key Features:

  • Advanced reinforcement learning for strategic combat
  • Simulation of various AI roles (Medic, Assassin, etc.)
  • Dynamic adaptation to different battle scenarios
  • Capability to handle up to six different character types with unique skills and items
