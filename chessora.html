Chessora

An innovative chess game combining classic chess with new strategic layers and gameplay mechanics.

Chessora Project Overview

Chessora brings a fresh twist to traditional chess by integrating strategic elements such as bounties, anthems, and unique piece abilities. Designed for both chess enthusiasts and new players, Chessora offers enhanced gameplay dynamics that increase depth and replayability. Players navigate through different phases involving anthem selection, shop management, and tactical decision-making on the chessboard.

The game is structured into several phases, including the beginning phase, shop phase, and end-of-round mechanics. Each phase introduces new strategic opportunities, allowing players to gain advantages and customize their play style.

Key Features:

  • Global Targets: Dynamic objectives that determine how gold is awarded at the end of each round.
  • Anthem Selection: Players choose from various anthems that provide unique abilities, such as additional moves or special piece powers.
  • Shop Phase: Buy, sell, or upgrade pieces, and acquire bounties and anthems to gain strategic advantages.
  • Unique Pieces: Introduces new piece types like Necromancer Pawn, Freeze Knight, and Disguise Rook, each with distinct abilities.

Game Phases:

  • Beginning Phase: Players select anthems and bounties to set their initial strategy.
  • Shop Phase: Manage your resources by purchasing new pieces or abilities from the shop.
  • End of Round: Players gain or lose gold based on the pieces captured or lost, influencing their strategy for subsequent rounds.

Unique Pieces and Abilities:

  • Necromancer Pawn: Revives captured pieces.
  • Freeze Knight: Freezes pieces it hops over for two turns.
  • Disguise Queen: Appears as a pawn and reveals its true form upon making a queen-like move.

Project Details

  • Technologies: Unity, C#, Custom Game Engine
  • Date: September 2024
  • Category: Game Development, Strategy
