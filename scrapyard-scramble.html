Scrapyard Scramble Project Overview

Scrapyard Scramble is a two-player card-drafting game designed to provide complex strategic choices. The game allows players to draft cards and build their deck from a shared card pool. Each game session offers a fresh and engaging experience as players adapt their strategies to outmaneuver their opponent.

The game also includes a “versus AI” mode, where players can challenge a computer opponent powered by a minimax algorithm. This mode offers an additional layer of difficulty and serves as a training tool for players looking to refine their skills.

Key Features:

  • Two-player card-drafting gameplay
  • AI opponent using a minimax algorithm
  • Strategic gameplay with diverse card options

Project Details
